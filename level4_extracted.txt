INTRODUCTION 
Chinese Proficiency Test (HSK), an international standardized test of Chinese 
language proficiency, assesses non-native Chinese speakers' abilities in using the 
Chinese language in their daily, academic and professional lives. HSK consists of 
six levels, namely HSK (level 1), HSK (level 2), HSK (level 3), HSK (level 4), 
HSK (level 5), and HSK (level 6). 
In all HSK levels, level 4 is a special level. Because if you want to seek 
application for the admission to Chinese Colleges, Such as Tsinghua University 
and Peking University, passing the HSK level 4 test is an essential requirement 
for foreign students. And it is also an essential requirement for foreigners to 
apply working or teaching in China. 
To pass New HSK level 4 test, the most important factor is to master the 1200 
vocabulary of level 4. After you master the 1200 words lists for new HSK level 
4, you can begin communicating with Chinese confidently when you study, work 
or live in a China. This book serves for the purpose to master the complete 1200 
HSK level 4 vocabulary words lists. 
The first section of the book gives complete vocabulary words list for HSK level 
4 with Pinyin and English notations. The book also gives a fully 1200 vocabulary 
summary at the last section, so you can always check back to the last section to 
see if you have truly mastered the level 4 vocabulary, even without the help of 
Pinyin and English notations.

passing the HSK level 4 test is an essential requirement for 
foreign students. And it is also an essential requirement for 
foreigners to apply working or teaching in China. 7

the 1200 words lists for new HSK level 4, you can begin com-
